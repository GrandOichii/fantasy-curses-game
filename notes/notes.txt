- learning spells (reading books) requires intelligence
- wielding heavy weaponry requires high strength
- mana regenerates over time
- some artifacts can increase mana regeneration
- you can buy books at the library
- upgrade shops by finding certain items while exploring
- range of ranged weapons is (range + DEX)
- whether you can begin an encounter is determined by your range + visible_range + 5
- opponents can also begin encounters
- cobwebs are burned using a fire source (spell, torch)
- if leveled enough, enemies will flee (or have an auto kill feature, not decided yet)
- when running in combat the player (and enemies) can fall, requiring to wait for a turn
- items/potions can grant statuses indefinately or temporarily
